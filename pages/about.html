What It Does

Nebula Scouting is a fast, opinionated scouting tool for FRC. Capture match data in seconds, surface team trends with sortable tables and charts, compare candidates side-by-side, and build your pick list by dragging teams into order.

2026 — REBUILT

This season tracks FUEL into the HUB during auto and teleop, plus Tower climbing (L1 / L2 / L3) in auto and endgame. Defense and breakdown flags are captured per match.
