Features

Everything you need to scout an event

Four tools, one workflow. Built for speed in the pits and clarity at the strategy table.

Match Scouting

Fast data entry

A streamlined match form built for the field. Track FUEL into the HUB, Tower climbing (L1 / L2 / L3), defense, and breakdowns — per match.

Data Review

Sortable history

Admins see every match scouted across the event; scouters see their own submissions. Sort any column instantly.

Team Comparison

Side-by-side

Compare any two teams across auto, teleop, and endgame averages. Pick decisions made on data, not vibes.

Pick List

Drag & drop

Admin-editable pick list backed by Firestore. Scouters get a live read-only view during alliance selection.

Workflow

From the field to the table in three steps

01

Capture

Scouters open the match form on their phones at the field. Tap counters, flag defense and breakdowns, hit submit.

02

Sync

Every submission writes straight to Firestore. No exporting, no syncing, no spreadsheet wrangling between matches.

03

Decide

Strategy leads open the dashboard, compare teams, and finalize the pick list — live, with everything that just happened.
